5 Signs Your Furnace Needs Service Before Edmonton's Winter

When the temperature in Edmonton drops toward -30°C, your furnace becomes the most important appliance in the house. A system that struggled through last winter will only work harder this year, driving up your gas bill and raising the risk of a mid-January breakdown. The good news is that most furnace failures give clear warning signs weeks or even months in advance. Here are the five to watch for before the cold really sets in.

Book a fall tune-up before the first cold snap. A clean, safely adjusted furnace is the single best insurance against a no-heat call at 2 a.m. JUJCO Heating & Cooling

The Benefits of Professional Furnace Servicing

A professional service visit goes well beyond a filter change. JUJCO technicians clean the burner and blower, inspect the heat exchanger for cracks, test safety controls, measure airflow, and verify combustion efficiency. Catching a hairline heat-exchanger crack early prevents both a carbon-monoxide risk and a full furnace replacement.

If your technician finds a problem, you will receive a clear assessment, an energy-efficiency reading, and a written estimate before any work begins. No surprises, no pressure, just the facts you need to decide.

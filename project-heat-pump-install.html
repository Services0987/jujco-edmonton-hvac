Cold-Climate Heat Pump Install

We installed a cold-climate heat pump for an Edmonton homeowner wanting efficient heating and cooling from one system.

Categories

  • Clients:

    The Fischer Family, Edmonton

  • Category:

    Heat Pump

  • Date:

    09 Apr, 2025

Project Challenges

The home needed both heating and cooling without ductwork in places. We paired a cold-climate outdoor unit with indoor heads and integrated the existing furnace as backup.

  1. The old single-stage furnace short-cycled and left the upper floor cold. We sized the replacement using a heat-loss calculation so the new system delivers steady, even heat across all levels.
  2. With the upgraded blower and a programmable thermostat, the homeowner gained quieter operation and lower winter gas bills.

Final Result

The family now enjoys efficient year-round comfort and lower utility bills, with the furnace stepping in only during extreme cold.

Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure which repair or upgrade is right for your home? Our free in-home consultation gives you honest, expert guidance tailored to your budget and Edmonton's climate.

What should I do in an HVAC emergency?

If your heating or cooling system stops working, leaks refrigerant, blows warm air, or makes loud noises, switch the unit off at the thermostat and breaker, then call our 24/7 emergency HVAC line for fast repair and installation support.

How often should I have my heating and cooling system serviced?

We recommend a professional tune-up twice a year — once in spring for your air conditioning and once in fall for your heating — to keep systems running efficiently, prevent unexpected breakdowns, and extend the life of your equipment.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency HVAC repair and installation?

Yes. Our certified technicians are available around the clock for heating and cooling emergencies, same-day repairs, and new system installations — just call +17802811000 and we will dispatch a technician right away.
